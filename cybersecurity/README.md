## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

> **Practice ethically ALWAYS.** Never hack systems without permission. Use legal practice platforms only.

> **Join CTFs regularly.** Competition experience is invaluable for learning and networking.

> **Document everything.** Keep detailed notes of your learning, techniques, and findings.

> **Get certified.** Certifications matter in cybersecurity. Start with Security+, progress to OSCP.
