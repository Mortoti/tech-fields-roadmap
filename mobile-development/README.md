## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Success

> **Test on real devices early.** Emulators don't show the full pictureâ€”use real phones whenever possible.

> **Master one framework deeply.** Don't jump between React Native, Flutter, and native. Pick one and become an expert.

> **Design matters in mobile.** Beautiful UI/UX can make or break a mobile app. Study popular apps and copy their patterns.

> **Start with Expo (React Native) or Flutter.** Don't struggle with native setup initially. Use tools that let you focus on learning.

> **Build for both platforms from day one.** Even if you prefer iOS, test on Android regularly (and vice versa).

> **Performance is critical.** Mobile users expect smooth 60fps. Learn optimization earlyâ€”lazy loading, memoization, list virtualization.

> **Understand app store guidelines.** Read App Store and Play Store policies before building. Some app ideas aren't allowed.

> **Publish early and often.** Don't wait for perfection. Get your first app in the stores to learn the process.
